Apartment Walkthrough

visualization

An interactive 3D apartment you can explore in first-person, built with Three.js.
Author

Andreas Bogossian

Published

April 19, 2026

A first-person walkthrough of a small apartment, built entirely from Three.js box primitives — no external models, no asset downloads. The living room and kitchen share an open-plan space; the bedroom is through a doorway on the right.

Click to explore
WASD or arrow keys to move  ·  Mouse to look
Esc to release the cursor
+

You start in the living room facing the kitchen. Walk forward to explore the kitchen area, turn around to find the sofa, or head right through the doorway into the bedroom.

The whole scene is a single {=html} block: no GLTF files, no build step — just Three.js box primitives arranged by hand into a believable space. A real production walkthrough would load a proper 3D model (from Blender, for example), but this shows how much is achievable with geometry alone.
